I'm Gulsheen Kaur Bhatia

UX Design Student

I'm Gulsheen Kaur Bhatia I was born in US and raised in Delhi, India. I am currently a UX design student in Wilfrid Laurier University, Ontario, Canada. I have always loved product and logo designing. My love for designing is what drove me into taking this course. I am beyond grateful that through this course, I’m working towards a dream of mine. Besides this, I am a voluntary Punjabi teacher at The Gurudwara - which is a Sikh place of worship. Pre COVID, the classes would be in person on the weekends however, now I teach online. This program teaches children their native language and culture through educational and creative means. When I’m not busy with school or helping out at the Gurdwara, you can find me doing some sort of artwork! One of my favourite pastimes is drawing animations. I’ve been doing it since I was 8! My others hobbies include watching movies, reading mangas, hanging out with family and friends, photography and listening to music. I take occasional trips, at least once a year to detach myself from the daily routine and recharge myself.
