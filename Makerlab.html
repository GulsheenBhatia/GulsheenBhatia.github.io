MAKER LAB 1

Colour Box

Summary

When designing and creating different projects sometimes picking out colours and looking for inspiration is difficult for some. The colour box is a way that designers can physically look at. Rather than picking and choosing different colours in the design to see what works well with one another you will be able to interact with the physical colours to pick and choose the colour you decide. You can change out different colours to figure out which work well together. This concept was created based off of the online version of colour schemes. Making the online version physical allows you to see an assortment of different colours rather than just a select few at a time. Seeing things in person allows designers to visualize what it will look like once it is finished.
