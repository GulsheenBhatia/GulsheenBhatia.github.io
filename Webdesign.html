Assignment 1

In this assignmnet, we learned about markdown and html. In this assignmnet we learned how to add images and use markdown. The scenario was that we met our friend from school after 5 years and we have a little time to catch up. We had to pick 4 categories for your goals. Mine were Family, Hobbies, Travel and University. We were given four markdown sheets where we put your categories as headings and talk about the important things that you have accomplished and want to tell it to your classmate. Add a picture realted to each category and use it in the markdown.

My Angle

Markdown is a text-to-HTML conversion tool for web writers. It allows you to write using an easy-to-read, easy-to-write plain text format, then convert it to structurally valid XHTML (or HTML). In the first markdown file I talked about family, how I spent time with my grandparents and made some really nice memories. I also got more close with my parents as I had more time to spent with them. Also talked about my brother who got married and how much fun I had planning his wedding. Then I talked about my hobbies that is watching anime and reading books. Also I talked about the collection of all the kpop albums I wanted. I love traveling and my dream place to visit was greece which I visited last year. This assignmnet made me think about my future goals and made me wonder how I would be after 5 years rom now. We also got to learn a lot about html like adding images and formatting the margins.
